The big bang theory

The series is a spin-off prequel to The Big Bang Theory and follows main character Sheldon Cooper growing up with his family in East Texas in the late 1980s and early 1990s.

Suits follows Mike Ross (Patrick J. Adams), who, despite never attending law school, is able to use his photographic memory to become a lawyer.
